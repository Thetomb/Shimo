Frequently Asked Questions

How to check my order status?

You can check your order status through the following methods:

  1. Log in to your Shimo account and check in "My Orders"
  2. Use the order number from your confirmation email to check on the "Order Inquiry" page
  3. Call customer service at 400-888-6789 with your order number

What is the warranty period for Shimo products?

Shimo smart standing desks provide industry-leading warranty service:

  • Motor and control system: 5-year warranty
  • Desk frame structure: 10-year warranty
  • Desktop material: 3-year warranty
  • Smart accessories: 2-year warranty

For detailed warranty policy, please refer to Warranty Terms

How to install Shimo standing desk?

Shimo standing desk adopts modular design for easy installation. You can:

  • Choose our professional installation service (free in some areas)
  • Install by yourself following the installation manual included with the product
  • Watch the installation video tutorial on our website

If you have any installation questions, please contact our customer service hotline for support.

What payment methods does Shimo standing desk support?

We support multiple payment methods, including:

  • Credit card/debit card payment
  • Alipay/WeChat Pay
  • Bank transfer
  • Installment payment (supports Huabei, JD Baitiao, etc.)

For corporate purchases, please contact our business team for more payment options.

How to apply for returns and exchanges?

We provide 7-day no-reason return and exchange service (product unused and packaging intact). The application process is as follows:

  1. Log in to your personal account and click "Apply for Return/Exchange" on the order details page
  2. Fill in the reason for return/exchange and submit
  3. Customer service staff will contact you within 24 hours to arrange return/exchange matters

Note: Customized products, installed or used products may not be applicable to the 7-day no-reason return and exchange policy. Please refer to Return and Exchange Policy for details.
