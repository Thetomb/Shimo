Product Description

The Shimo Smart Standing Desk Pro Max is our flagship product, combining the latest ergonomic design with smart technology. Features a high-strength aerospace aluminum frame and premium solid wood desktop, supporting electric height adjustment and intelligent memory functions.

Key Features:

  • Smart Height Memory: Store 4 commonly used height settings, one-click switching
  • Ultra-quiet Motor: Lifting noise below 40 decibels
  • Anti-collision Protection: Automatically stops when encountering obstacles
  • USB Charging Ports: Desktop integrated with 3 USB fast charging ports
  • Wireless Charging Zone: Supports Qi standard wireless charging

Frequently Asked Questions

Is the installation of this standing desk complicated?

+

Our product design considers installation convenience, and most customers report they can complete the installation within 30-60 minutes. We also provide professional installation service (additional fee required), which you can select when placing your order.

Is the lifting process noisy?

+

The Pro Max model uses ultra-quiet motor technology with lifting noise below 40 decibels, equivalent to library environment noise level, which won't disturb your work or rest.

What's the warranty period for this product?

+

We provide a 5-year warranty for the frame and motor, and a 2-year warranty for the desktop and electronic components. Our customer service team is available 24/7 to assist with any issues.

Can I control the desk with my smartphone?

+

Yes, our Shimo app is available for both iOS and Android devices. You can control height adjustments, set reminders to stand/sit, and monitor your daily activity patterns through the app.

What's the maximum weight this desk can support?

+

The Pro Max model can support up to 265 lbs (120kg) of evenly distributed weight, which is sufficient for multiple monitors, computers, and other office equipment.
